## Dedicated billing (now complete)
- Trimmed the Dedicated rate sheet to the **5 rows that bill**: `ANNUAL_RATE`, `DAY_RATE`, `MONTHLY`
  (day-rate basis, priority Day>Annual>Monthly), `OT_HOURLY_RATE`, `WEEKEND_HOURLY_RATE`. Removed dead/legacy
  (`HOURLY_BUSINESS/OOB/WEEKEND`, `MONTHLY_DAY_RATE`, `HOURLY_BACKFILL`).
- **Hourly basis added, PER-TECHNICIAN** (`Technician.dedicatedBillingBasis` = DAY_RATE | HOURLY; the account
  toggle was tried then removed). HOURLY = regular hours × `HOURLY` rate (+ OT + weekend). A band-2 day-rate
  tech and a band-2 hourly tech share one account card. `fte-rows.ts` branches per assignment's technician.
- **OT vs OOB (key distinction):** OT = overtime (>defaultHours/day), **Dedicated only**. OOB = after-hours
  time-of-day, **Dispatch only** (Dedicated has no clock data). Not the same thing.
- Backfill vs No-Backfill = the tier toggle (slaId). Rebadged = per-tech fields, ignores the account card.
